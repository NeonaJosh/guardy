## 💬 Likely Judge Questions

### "Is the AI real?"
**Answer:** "Watch - I'll change to Hindi and request an AI reply. The system will call OpenAI and generate a real Hindi response in real-time. Not templated, not pre-written."

### "What's the Auth0 integration?"
**Answer:** "Click Teams. Create a team. That's creating a real Auth0 Organization via our M2M backend authentication. This is how enterprise SaaS handles multi-tenancy."

### "How is this different?"
**Answer:** "Most AI tools ask 'forgiveness' after the fact. Guardy AI asks for 'permission' first. Users control what AI can do via the Token Vault. If Send is OFF, AI can't send. See? [Toggle OFF, try to reply, gets blocked]"

### "Audit logging?"
**Answer:** "Click the Audit Log button. Downloads complete action history with timestamp and user ID. Perfect for compliance and accountability."

### "How does it scale?"
**Answer:** "Unlimited teams via Auth0 Organizations. Each team is isolated. Permission-based RBAC means we can scale to thousands of teams instantly."
